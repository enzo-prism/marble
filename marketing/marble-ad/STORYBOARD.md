# Marble 9:16 Video Ad Storyboard

## Strategy

Audience: lifters and hybrid athletes who want a fast, private training log without turning their workout history into another noisy social dashboard.

Core promise: Marble is the calm local layer for logging sets, importing chosen workout summaries, reviewing progress, and keeping the data on-device.

Rhythm: hook-hold, FAST logging, intentional import, progress reveal, quiet CTA.

## Beat 1: Local Logbook

Time: 0.0-3.2

Concept: A black marble field opens like a disciplined training notebook. The real Journal screen floats in, grounded by the line "Your training history, without the noise."

Message: Marble is calm, private, and centered on training history.

Visuals: Real dark-mode Journal snapshot in a phone frame, monochrome type, marble-vein rules, ghost typography.

## Beat 2: Fast Set Logging

Time: 2.9-6.45

Concept: The workout does not pause for a database app. The Log Set screen slides in with crisp metric callouts that make the interaction feel fast and physical.

Message: Log weight, reps, RPE, rest, notes, and PR targets quickly.

Visuals: Real Add Set snapshot, "185 lb" and "PR" metric cards, Save Set callout.

## Beat 3: Intentional Import

Time: 6.0-9.45

Concept: Imported workouts become chosen records, not silent clutter. A real calendar/progress-media screen anchors the result while provider rows explain the source model.

Message: Apple Health, Garmin via Health, and Strava can feed the journal through sanctioned, user-chosen paths.

Visuals: Calendar/progress-media snapshot, provider panel for Health, Garmin via Health, Strava.

## Beat 4: Progress Surfaces

Time: 9.1-12.55

Concept: Trends turns training data into a readable pattern. The screenshot scrolls just enough to show that Marble has more depth than a single logging card.

Message: See consistency, volume, exercise progress, supplements, and PRs.

Visuals: Real Trends snapshot, tag row with key analysis surfaces.

## Beat 5: Private By Default

Time: 12.25-16.0

Concept: The ad ends quietly, not with hype. Marble's app icon and the local-only privacy claims become the final trust signal.

Message: Private workout + supplements journal. Stored on your iPhone. No Marble server.

Visuals: App icon, huge CTA type, three trust lines.
