# Marble 2.1 review notes

Marble does not require an account or sign-in. All core workout, supplement,
planning, trend, and backup data is stored locally on the device. The app has no
ads or third-party tracking.

## What is new

- Session-led Workout tab with start, repeat set, add set, and finish flows.
- Sprint prescriptions with distance, repetition count, target time or range,
  rest, and immutable per-rep result snapshots.
- Redesigned Exercise Library with search, favorites, safe editing, and a choice
  of custom emoji or SF Symbol for each exercise.
- JSON backup and restore for exercises, sets, supplements, workout sessions,
  sprint plans/results, and the weekly split. Progress photos and videos are
  intentionally excluded and remain on the device.
- Weight-unit normalization across history and progress comparisons.

## Suggested review path

1. Open **Workout**, tap **Start Workout**, add or repeat a set, then finish it.
2. Open **Workout > Data & Backups > Exercise Library** to edit an exercise and
   choose an emoji or SF Symbol.
3. Create or edit a **Sprint** exercise to add a sprint prescription, then log a
   rep and review the saved target in Journal.
4. Open **Workout > Data & Backups** to inspect export and restore.
5. Open **Trends** after logging sets to review progress and records.

Apple Health access is optional. Apple Watch and Garmin workouts can be imported
through Apple Health. Strava is hidden unless developer credentials are present.
No credentials are needed for review.
